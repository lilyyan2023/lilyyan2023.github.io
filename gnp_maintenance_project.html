Problem

Our team worked with Good News Partners (GNP) to help them transition from recording employees’ timesheets on paper to digitally manage timesheets and approve them.

My Role

I was the product manager of the whole team, so I would manage team’s progress, define business objectives, work with designers, engineers in the team, and communicate with the client to ask for feedback.

Solution

Our team designed and implemented the website that employees can log in to record their working time and submit the time bi-weekly for managers’ approval. Managers can log in their side of website to check and approve the submitted timesheets.

Result

Our team successfully implemented the product with a full-functional extent and I also handed off the project to our client.
