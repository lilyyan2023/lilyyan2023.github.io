Facebook Data Challenge

Tools Used

Data Analytics
Data Visualization
R
SQL

Problem

I participated in Facebook Data Challenge final round and the problem was to analyze trends in the content Netflix’s adding to their catalog to know what Zuckflix should launch first.

My Role

I worked independently on this and I used SQL to analyze data trends and R to visualize data. Then, I put together a powerpoint to present my findings using visualization.

Solution

Our team designed a game to allow adolescents to play around and learned new financial knowledge at the same time. Because of time constraint, we wasn’t able to fully implement the game, but we were able to present a compelling pitch deck to PNC bank judges.

Result

I was one of the finalists for Facebook Data Challenge and my work was highly complimented by judges and mentors.
