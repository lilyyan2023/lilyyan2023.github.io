Trix4Kids

Tools Used

Product Management
Python Flask
Business Pitch

Problem

Our team participated in PNC bank hackathon and we need to develop a product to improve adolescents’ financial awareness within 2 days.

My Role

I was the product manager of the whole team, so I would facilitate brainstorming, design solutions, manage team’s progress, work with engineers in the team, and put up together a business pitch to present to our client at the end of the hackathon.

Solution

Our team designed a game to allow adolescents to play around and learned new financial knowledge at the same time. Because of time constraint, we wasn’t able to fully implement the game, but we were able to present a compelling pitch deck to PNC bank judges.

Result

Our team was awarded top 5 in the hackathon.
