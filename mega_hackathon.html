Mega Hackathon

Website Link

Tools Used

Product Management
Pitch
Event Organization
Marketing

Problem

Blockchain Hackathon was previously only held by companies and there was no a large online blockchain hackathon that was held by schools only. It made the barrier to entry these hackathons higher and potentially restricted people with limited web3 knowledge's exploration.

My Role

I was one of the main organizers of Mega Hackathon. My other teammates and I created pitch deck and found sponsors, speakers, workshop leads. We also outreached many different schools across the globe for marketing partners. Besides, I was responsible of settling down on logistics of the whole event. I also managed the official website of our hackathon.

Solution

I, representing Northwestern Blockchain Group, co-hosted with USC CSSA Mega Hackathon. It was a 4-week fully remote online hackathon and open to all to participate.

Result

Our team was able to raise 70k prize pool in total and collaborated with over 20+ marketing partners across the globe. The whole event was able to attract 200+ participants and 50+ project submissions.
